the spacbar. is a Johannesburg-based production and creative agency founded by Simz Mahlangu — Film Director, Editor, Photographer, Graphic Designer and Creative Director.

We work at the brutal edge of culture and storytelling, translating vision into work that moves, resonates and endures. From Netflix trailers to national brand campaigns, we do not decorate. We create.
